What is Salsa Fest at UVa? 1 day, 8 workshops, and 1 awesome party!

University Salsa Fest is the third year of UVa's annual Salsa Fest. Come learn with us for a day of 8 workshops featuring instructors from all over the east coast, including Latin Vintage and DC Style Salsa Academy, and then get ready to party with DJ Steve Greene and live salsa band Conjunto Sason!
