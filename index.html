What is University Salsa Fest? 1 day, 9 workshops, and 1 awesome party!

Working in conjunction with locals to bring the best of Virginia's salsa scene together in Charlottesville, the University Salsa Club presents the University Salsa Fest! The University Salsa Fest is a statewide event that brings together both student and non-student salsa enthusiasts for a day of lessons, workshops, and social dancing. Cheap to students and non-students, the workshops are hosted by salsa gurus from Charlottesville, Northern Virginia, Richmond, DC, Philly, Atlanta, and beyond, including some UVA Alumni, faculty and staff!

Directions from DC: Take I-66W until Exit 43A and merge on to US-29S/Lee Hwy. Continue for 69 miles, make a slight right onto Stadium Road. Take the 2nd right onto Whitehead Road, turn left onto George Welsh Way. The SAB will be on the corner of George Welsh and Alderman Road.

Directions from Richmond: Take I-64W, follow signs for I-64W/I-95N/Charlottesville/Washington and merge onto I-95 N. Make a slight right onto I-64 W. Take Exit 118B to merge onto US-29 N toward Charlottesville/Culpeper. Take the US-29 BUS N exit toward Charlottesville, turn right onto US-29 Bus N/Fontaine Ave. Turn left onto Maury Ave, continue on Maury as it turns into Alderman. The SAB will be on the right, at the intersection of Alderman and George Welsh.
